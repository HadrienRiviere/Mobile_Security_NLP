13 Safety
PLACEMENT GUIDELINES 
To avoid risk of overheating and burns:
Use only in temperatures between –40° and 113°F 
(–40° to 45°C)  
Do not block airflow to Nest Cam IQ 
To avoid risk of fire and shock:
Use only provided power cable and power adapter 
Install in accordance with building and installation 
codes where you live 
To avoid risk of other serious injury:
Keep camera and all components out of the reach 
of children and pets 
For more information on safety go to nest com/camsafety
